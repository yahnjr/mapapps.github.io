Medford Transportation Survey

Use the interactive mapping tool below to identify specific locations in Medford where you feel transportation safety improvements are needed and suggest ideas for enhancements. Click on locations of interest, like roads, intersections and crosswalks, to leave comments and see feedback from others in the community. Multiple comments are welcome!

When placing a pin on the map and sharing your comment, consider:

  • What transportation safety concerns do you have in your neighborhood or areas where you travel most frequently? Consider the following types of concerns when providing your comment:
    • Near Miss
    • Not Enough Separation
    • Distracted Driving
    • Insufficient Lighting
    • Speeding
    • Line of Sight
    • Other
  • What are your ideas for improving these transportation safety issues?

Do you have any additional general comments?
