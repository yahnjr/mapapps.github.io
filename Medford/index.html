Medford Transportation Survey

Get Involved: Take the Phase 1 Survey!

Use the interactive mapping tool below to identify specific locations in Medford where you have transportation safety concerns, feel transportation safety improvements are needed, and/or to suggest ideas for enhancements. Click on locations of interest, like roads, intersections, and crosswalks, to leave comments and see feedback from others in the community. Multiple comments are welcome!

When placing a pin on the map and sharing your comment, consider:

  • Are there specific locations where you feel unsafe driving, walking, biking, skateboarding, scootering or one-wheeling? Please mark these areas on the map below.
  • Have you observed unsafe behaviors by people using roadways in your neighborhood or those you travel most frequently? Please mark these on the map below.
  • What changes would make you feel safer when traveling in your neighborhood or community? Please describe them in the comment box below.
